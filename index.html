Clair de Lune

About

Clair de Lune is a classical-jazz musical trio consisting of piano, violin and soprano (Nathaniel, Judy, and Bregitte). The three met each other whilst they were studying a Bachelor of Music at the Australian Institute of Music, and went on to experiment in a variety of genres. They love performing in a variety of locations for weddings, corporate events and parties and have even gone busking in the Botanical Gardens (The spot they chose in the garden nearly resulted them being run over by the reversing garbage truck!) They love to bring popular pieces into each program to allow a delightful bite size concert full of memories and beautiful sounds.
